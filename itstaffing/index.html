IT Staffing

Legal Compliance

We make it easy for contractors to enjoy the same benefits as full-time employees—without the complications of traditional labour contracts. As your Employer of Record (EOR) partner, we also simplify the process of hiring and managing global talent on your behalf.

Local Support

We deliver end-to-end local support to help your contract staff feel truly connected to your company culture. From onboarding to ongoing care, our services include occupational health support and initiatives that promote a positive, productive work environment for your technical teams.

Attract More Talent

As a specialised IT staffing agency, we connect top talent with meaningful career opportunities and competitive compensation. This allows us to offer our clients a strong pipeline of skilled professionals who are both motivated and well-matched to their needs.

Direct Relationships

Work side by side with top-tier software engineers who become an integral part of your daily project operations. Our full-time developers embed seamlessly into your teams, boosting code quality, productivity, and delivery speed. Behind the scenes, we ensure smooth communication and strong cultural alignment for long-term success.

Extra Available

We make it easy by offering computer rentals and additional equipment as part of your service package — conveniently bundled into a single invoice and tailored to your specific requirements.
